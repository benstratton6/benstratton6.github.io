Welcome to the On Call Africa Interactive Map

This map shows the districts within the Southern and Western provinces of Zambia in which On Call Africa is currently active. Under the programs section you will find the 3 different projects we are either coordinating or partnering in. Zoom in on the highlighted districts to find out more details about individual health centres within them that we are working with.

Use the intresting features tab to discover more health realted infastructive in our area; the provinces tab to view Western and Southern provinces individually and find out the populations of their districts; and use the base maps tab to swap between street view and satellite view.

This map was made with assistance from the team at GIS-E.
